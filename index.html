Are your kids struggling to grasp the concept of money? Have no fear, because we're gonna help you!

Log in below, or read the instructions...

How it works:

  1. Create a parent account by signing in!
  2. Add a child account using the "Add Child" function.
  3. Make sure you've added the child before letting them sign in to the site using the email you used to add them.
As a parent, you can create tasks, which will reward your child(ren) with coins. You can also add prizes, where your child(ren) will be able to purchase the prizes you've uploaded for coins earned through tasks.

As a child, you can earn coins by completing tasks and purchase prizes from your parent.
